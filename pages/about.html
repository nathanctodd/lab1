About the Photographer
Nathan Todd
Nathan Todd is a full-time student at Brigham Young University currently studying Applied and Computational Mathematics with a minor in Computer Science and a concentration in Data Science and Machine Learning. Nathan also is a 7-year photographer who focuses his work on landscapes and nature. He also enjoys running and skiing. He lives in Utah with his gorgeous wife and 4 kids.
